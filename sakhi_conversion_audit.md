## 2. Most Damaging Conversion Weaknesses

### ðŸš¨ WEAKNESS 1: The "Identity Crisis" (What is Sakhi?)
Visitors understand Sakhi is a "companion," but they don't know the **form factor**.
- **The Risk:** People fear downloading new apps or signing up for things. If they think they have to download a 100MB app to get a 3 AM answer, they will leave.
- **The Fix:** Use a "Mechanism Descriptor" in the H1 or Sub-headline.
    - *Current:* "Meet Sakhi - Your Compassionate Companion."
    - *Better:* "Meet Sakhi - Your AI Companion for Fertility Support. **Available 24/7 on WhatsApp.**"

### ðŸš¨ WEAKNESS 2: The "Conversion Leak" (The Knowledge Hub)
The secondary button in the Hero section ("Browse Knowledge Hub") gives users an "easy exit." 
- **The Risk:** Users who are "almost ready" to chat will click the Hub to "learn more," lose momentum, and never start the conversation.
- **The Fix:** Remove the secondary CTA from the Hero. Every ounce of attention must go to "Start Talking."

### ðŸš¨ WEAKNESS 3: Platform Ambiguity
The floating button is WhatsApp, but the Demo is Web-based.
- **The Risk:** Users develop "Switching Anxiety." *Wait, will this open my WhatsApp? I wanted to stay private on the web.*
- **The Fix:** Explicitly label the handover. "Try the web demo below, or message us on WhatsApp for permanent support."
